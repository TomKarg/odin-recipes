Skyr bowl with granola and fruit

Description

Treat yourself to an easy and delicious skyr bowl with granola that feels almost like dessert for breakfast. Creamy vanilla skyr, crunchy granola, sweet banana, juicy berries, and nutty chia seeds come together to create a burst of flavours that will satisfy your taste buds and give you a wholesome start to the day.

  • 250 ml Skyr vanilla
  • 3 tbsp Granola
  • 100 g Strawberries, raspberries, and / or blueberries
  • 1 Small banana, sliced
  • Â½ tsp Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Pour vanilla skyr into a bowl.
  2. Spread granola, berries, banana, and chia seeds on top.
  3. Garnish with edible flowers and serve.
